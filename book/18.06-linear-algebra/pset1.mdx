**Problem 1.** Find a solution for $x$, $y$, $z$ to the system of equations

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 2 & 3\\
4 & 5 & 6\\
7 & 8 & 10\\
\end{bmatrix}

\begin{bmatrix}
x\\
y\\
z\\
\end{bmatrix}

= 

\begin{bmatrix}
3e + \pi + 2\sqrt{2}\\
6e + 4\pi + 5\sqrt{2}\\
10e + 7\pi + 8\sqrt{2}\\
\end{bmatrix}
$$

<details>
<summary>Solution.</summary>

Answer:

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
x\\
y\\
z\\
\end{bmatrix}
 
= 

\begin{bmatrix}
\pi\\
\sqrt{2}\\
e\\
\end{bmatrix}
$$

Solution:
Looking at the column picture, we see that we can rewrite the system of equations as follows:

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
1\\
4\\
7\\
\end{bmatrix}
x

+ 

\begin{bmatrix}
2\\
5\\
8\\
\end{bmatrix}
y

+

\begin{bmatrix}
3\\
6\\
10\\
\end{bmatrix}
z

= 

\begin{bmatrix}
3e + \pi + 2\sqrt{2}\\
6e + 4\pi + 5\sqrt{2}\\
10e + 7\pi + 8\sqrt{2}\\
\end{bmatrix}
$$

From this, we can see the answer.
